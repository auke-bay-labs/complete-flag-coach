# App Store Screenshots Guide

## Required Sizes
- **iPhone 6.7"** (1290x2796) — iPhone 15 Pro Max / 16 Pro Max
- **iPhone 6.1"** (1179x2556) — iPhone 15 / 16
- **iPad 13"** (2064x2752) — iPad Pro 13"
- **Apple Watch** (optional, 410x502 for Series 9/Ultra)

## How to Capture

### Method: Simulator Screenshots
1. Run the app from Xcode on each simulator size
2. In Simulator: **File > Screenshot** (Cmd+S)
3. Screenshots save to Desktop

### Recommended Screens (5 per device)

**Screenshot 1: Playbook Grid**
- Open app → Playbook tab → Grid view
- Show 6-8 play cards visible
- Caption: "30+ PLAYS READY TO GO"

**Screenshot 2: Play Detail (Huddle Mode)**
- Tap any play to open full-screen huddle view
- Dark background, big play diagram
- Caption: "SHOW THE PLAY IN THE HUDDLE"

**Screenshot 3: Live Scoreboard**
- Game Day tab → Live
- Set some scores (e.g., 19-12), Q3, 2nd & 7
- Clock running
- Caption: "RUN YOUR GAME FROM THE SIDELINE"

**Screenshot 4: Roster & Rotation**
- Scroll down on Live to show player rotation
- Some players on field, playing time bars visible
- Caption: "TRACK EVERY PLAYER'S TIME"

**Screenshot 5: Apple Watch**
- Show the watch scoreboard alongside the iPhone
- Caption: "SCORE AND CLOCK ON YOUR WRIST"

### For iPad Screenshots
Same 5 screens but in landscape orientation to show the wider layout.

### Adding Captions
Use a tool like Figma, Canva, or Apple's Marketing Assets to add text overlays:
- Font: SF Pro Display Bold (or Barlow Condensed to match the app)
- Colors: Navy (#0a1930) background, White text, Cyan (#00c8ff) accent
- Place caption at top, screenshot below

## App Store Preview Video (30 seconds)

### Storyboard

| Time | Screen | Action |
|------|--------|--------|
| 0-3s | Sign-in screen | "Your sideline command center" text fades in |
| 3-7s | Playbook grid | Scroll through plays, tap one |
| 7-11s | Huddle mode | Play diagram fills screen, animation runs |
| 11-15s | Live scoreboard | Start clock, add touchdown (+6), watch clock tick |
| 15-19s | Player rotation | Tap players on/off field, time bars update |
| 19-22s | Apple Watch | Show watch with matching score and clock |
| 22-26s | Team code share | QR code screen, "Share with your team" |
| 26-30s | App icon + tagline | "Complete Flag Coach — Download free" |

### How to Record
1. In Simulator: **File > Record Screen**
2. Navigate through each screen per the storyboard
3. Use QuickTime to trim and combine clips
4. Export at the required resolution
5. Upload to App Store Connect under "App Preview"

### Required Video Specs
- iPhone 6.7": 1290x2796 or 886x1920
- iPad 13": 2064x2752 or 1200x1600
- Duration: 15-30 seconds
- Format: H.264, .mov or .mp4
- No device frame (Apple adds it)
