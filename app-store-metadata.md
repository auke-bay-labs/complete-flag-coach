## Description

Complete Flag Coach is the all-in-one flag football coaching app built for coaches who want to spend less time drawing plays and more time coaching their team.

**Playbook**
Browse a library of built-in formations — Shotgun, Spread, Bunch, Trips, and Wishbone — with pass, run, and trick plays ready to go. Every play includes animated route diagrams, position assignments, and coaching tips. Edit any play to make it your own, or build completely custom plays from scratch with the visual play builder.

**Game Day**
Run your game from the sideline with the live scoreboard — track the clock, score, downs, timeouts, and possession all in one place. Use the Play Caller to tap through your playbook in real time and flash plays to your team on the big screen.

**Apple Watch**
Glance at the score and clock right from your wrist. The watchOS companion syncs with your iPhone in real time — start the clock on your phone and see it ticking on your watch. Track scores, downs, timeouts, and the play clock without pulling your phone out of your pocket.

**Practice Planning**
Organize practice sessions with structured plans. Focus drills on specific formations or situations so your team is ready on game day.

**Print & Share**
Print wristband cards for your players, full coach playbooks, or share individual play diagrams. Export your entire playbook as a backup or to share with assistant coaches.

No ads. No subscriptions. No account required. Just football.
