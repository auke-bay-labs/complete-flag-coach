Privacy Policy

Complete Flag Coach — Last updated: April 18, 2026

Complete Flag Coach ("the App") is a flag football coaching tool designed for coaches, players, and teams. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App does not require an account, does not ask for your name or email, and does not use analytics or advertising frameworks.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Play diagrams, formations, and coaching notes you create
  • Game scores, schedules, and season history you enter
  • App preferences and settings

This data stays on your device and is under your control.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, the App uses Apple's CloudKit service to sync your playbook data across your devices (iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch). This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Apple Watch

The App includes a watchOS companion that syncs game clock and score data with the iPhone app in real time using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This communication happens directly between your devices and does not pass through any external server.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children. The App contains no user accounts, social features, or in-app purchases.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
