Frequently Asked Questions

How do I invite parents and coaches to my team?

After creating a team, you'll see a 6-character team code and QR code. Share the code via text, email, or show the QR code at practice. Parents can scan it or type it in to join.

Do parents need to create an account?

No. Parents can tap "Join with Team Code" on the sign-in screen and enter the code without creating an Apple ID account. They get read-only access to the playbook and live scoreboard.

Does the Apple Watch app sync with the iPad?

Yes. The watch syncs through your iPhone via WatchConnectivity. Start the clock on the iPad, and it appears on the watch in real time. Your iPhone needs to be nearby (in your pocket works).

Can I change the game clock settings?

Yes. Tap the gear icon on the Live scoreboard to configure quarter length (8-30 min), number of periods (2 halves or 4 quarters), timeouts per half, and play clock duration.

How does playing time tracking work?

On the Live scoreboard, scroll down to Player Rotation. Tap players to mark them as on-field. When the game clock runs, on-field players automatically accumulate playing time. Color-coded bars show who needs more time.

Is my data backed up?

Yes. All plays, rosters, and game data sync automatically via iCloud. Your data is available on all your devices signed into the same Apple ID.

Can I print play cards for my players?

Yes. Go to More > Wristband & Print to create wristband cards, coach playbooks, or blank play card templates.
